Our Story

A Brooklyn Institution, Baked with Love.

Since 1965, Di Fara Pizza has been serving up some of the most celebrated slices in New York City from the same no-frills Midwood storefront on Avenue J.

Every pie is crafted with the same dedication it has always been — fresh ingredients, housemade sauce, and the kind of care you can only put into something you've been perfecting for decades.

"Crust was right, sauce tasted homemade — just the right amount of cheese."

Whether you're a Brooklyn local or making a dedicated pilgrimage, the wait is always worth it.
